Promoters' Message

Groma Global Limited is pleased to present our updates as we embark on our journey toward establishing an Initial Public Offering (IPO).

To Our Shareholders & Potential Investors,

"Since our inception in 2006, Groma Global has remained steadfast in our goal to bridge agricultural production hubs with markets worldwide. Over the past 19 years, we have scaled our sourcing, warehousing, and processing networks across India, Africa, Canada, and beyond, delivering premium grains, pulses, and seeds to our global customer base.

As we enter this exciting next chapter as a public enterprise, we commit to reinforcing our standards of corporate governance, transparency, and shareholder value creation. Our expansion into public markets will fuel our infrastructure enhancements and logistic efficiencies, cementing our status as a trusted, state-of-the-art agro commodity partner. We thank you for your continued trust and invite you to be part of our growth."

The Promoters Groma Global Limited
