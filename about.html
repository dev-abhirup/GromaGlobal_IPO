About Us

Global Agro Commodity Experts You Can Trust

Company Profile

The company has been engaged in importing pulses from Singapore, Burma, Tanzania, Ukraine, Sri Lanka, Hong Kong, Canada, Australia, and Middle Eastern countries, and supplying them to its regular clients in Delhi, Indore, Chennai, Cochin, Jalgaon, and across India for the past 16 years.

We primarily focus on the trading of premium quality pulses, grains, seeds, and food products. Our operations are fully equipped with the necessary manpower and machinery to meet the specific requirements of both buyers and sellers.

We assist our clients in securing the best possible bids, enabling them to achieve maximum efficiency in their daily trading activities. Our team stays updated with the latest market trends and acts as a catalyst between originators, trade houses, and millers.

We have the capability to cater to client requirements, regardless of size or volume, and our principal aim is to offer products that are high in quality and purity.

Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To lead the company into key global markets, laying the groundwork for a strong foray into international trade and establishing a trusted presence in major trade hubs worldwide, including India, Canada, Tanzania, and Malawi.

Our Mission

Driven by the philosophy of "Work is Worship" ("कर्म ही पूजा है"), we operate with unwavering dedication, strong principles, and relentless determination to remain a trusted name in global commodities trading and reach new heights of success.

Our Services

Our services in the agro trading creates value addition to customer in terms of sourcing of quality, commodity, saving in time, reduced costs, time scheduled delivery, etc. The GGL’s technical and marketing personnel ensure that the quality of every product meets the standard that they have set for themselves. Its quality control engineers are pivotal in manufacturing high quality products that maximize their clients’ satisfaction.

GGL provides comprehensive logical solutions. GGL’s services also include transportation, warehousing, cold storages and distribution. Its services can be integrated together to deliver powerful, end-to-end supply chain solutions that will help the clients achieve their strategic business objectives while driving costs out of the supply.

Indeed, GGL has the capability to cater to its clients requirements regardless of their size or volume requirements. Company’s principal aim is to offer products that are high in quality and purity.
